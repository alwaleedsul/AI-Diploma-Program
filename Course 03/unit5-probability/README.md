# Unit 5: Probability Theory and Statistical Inference
## نظرية الاحتمالات والاستدلال الإحصائي

### Learning Objectives | أهداف التعلم

By the end of this unit, students will be able to:
- Apply probability theory and statistical inference to ML models
- Understand and apply Bayesian inference methods
- Apply central limit theorem with simulations
- Implement maximum likelihood estimation (MLE)
- Implement hypothesis testing procedures
- Compute p-values and confidence intervals
- Connect probability theory to ML model implementations

---

## Unit Duration | مدة الوحدة

**Theoretical Hours:** 6 hours  
**Practical Hours:** 6 hours  
**Total Hours:** 12 hours

**Format:** 6 theoretical + 6 practical hours

---

## Topics Covered | المواضيع المغطاة

1. **Probability Distributions**
2. **Statistical Inference**
3. **Bayesian Inference**
4. **Central Limit Theorem**
5. **Sampling and Point Estimation**
6. **Maximum Likelihood Estimation**
7. **Hypothesis Testing**
8. **P-values and Confidence Intervals**
