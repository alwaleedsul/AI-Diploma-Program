### AIAT 126 - Graduation Project
- **Credit Hours:** 3
- **Lecture Hours:** 1
- **Practical Hours:** 4
- **Tutorial Hours:** 0
- **Contact Hours:** 5
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Units:** 5 units
- **Total Hours:** 80 (16 theoretical + 64 practical)

**Description:**
This course represents the culmination of the student's learning experience in the AI Diploma program. It typically involves applying AI techniques, tools, and methodologies to solve a real-world problem or explore an innovative idea. The project demonstrates the student's ability to integrate knowledge from multiple domains such as machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and data science in a practical application.

**Learning Objectives (CLOs):**
- CLO1: Design and develop a comprehensive AI solution to address a specific real-world problem
- CLO2: Integrate knowledge from different AI subfields into a coherent and practical system
- CLO3: Conduct critical evaluation of implemented solution performance using relevant metrics
- CLO4: Effectively communicate project goals, processes, results, and impacts through technical documentation
- CLO5: Identify and analyze ethical, legal, and social considerations relevant to the AI solution

**Unit Breakdown:**
1. Unit 1: Project Proposal and Plan (4 theoretical + 10 practical)
2. Unit 2: Solution System Design and Architecture (3 theoretical + 12 practical)
3. Unit 3: Project Implementation and Development (3 theoretical + 14 practical)
4. Unit 4: Evaluation and Refinement (3 theoretical + 14 practical)
5. Unit 5: Project Documentation and Final Presentation (3 theoretical + 14 practical)

**Key Requirements:**
- Must solve a REAL-WORLD problem (مشكلة واقعية)
- Must integrate knowledge from multiple AI subfields
- Must include ethical considerations
- Must have professional documentation
- Must demonstrate practical application skills
