## Overview

This document outlines safety procedures and requirements for all AI Diploma program activities, including lab work, practical exercises, and project development.

---

## 1. Workspace Safety | سلامة بيئة العمل

### 1.1 Workspace Organization

**Requirements:**
- ✅ Maintain clean, organized, and well-lit workspace
- ✅ Keep work area free of clutter and unnecessary items
- ✅ Organize cables and equipment properly
- ✅ Ensure adequate lighting for screen work
- ✅ Maintain proper ventilation

**Best Practices:**
- Clean workspace before and after each session
- Organize files and materials systematically
- Use cable management solutions
- Ensure comfortable seating and ergonomic setup

---

## 2. Equipment Safety | سلامة المعدات

### 2.1 Computer Equipment

**Requirements:**
- ✅ Keep food and drinks away from work area
- ✅ Avoid opening power sources and touching hot or high-voltage areas
- ✅ Handle equipment with care
- ✅ Report damaged equipment immediately

**Guidelines:**
- No food or drinks within 1 meter of computer equipment
- Do not attempt to repair equipment yourself
- Unplug equipment before cleaning
- Use surge protectors for all electronic devices

### 2.2 Power and Electrical Safety

**Critical Rules:**
- ⚠️ **NEVER** open power sources
- ⚠️ **NEVER** touch hot or high-voltage areas
- ⚠️ **NEVER** use damaged cables or adapters
- ✅ Always use proper grounding
- ✅ Check cables for damage before use

**Emergency Procedures:**
- If you see sparks or smell burning: **STOP IMMEDIATELY**
- Unplug equipment safely
- Notify instructor/supervisor
- Do not attempt to fix electrical issues yourself

---

## 3. Emergency Procedures | إجراءات الطوارئ

### 3.1 Fire Safety

**Requirements:**
- ✅ Know locations of fire extinguishers and first aid tools
- ✅ Know emergency exits
- ✅ Understand fire evacuation procedures

**Fire Extinguisher Locations:**
- Check with your instructor for specific locations in your workspace
- Familiarize yourself with the type of extinguisher (ABC type recommended)

**In Case of Fire:**
1. **Alert:** Notify others immediately
2. **Evacuate:** Leave the area using nearest exit
3. **Assemble:** Meet at designated assembly point
4. **Report:** Inform instructor/supervisor

### 3.2 First Aid

**First Aid Kit Locations:**
- Check with your instructor for specific locations
- Know basic first aid procedures

**Common Issues:**
- **Minor cuts:** Clean with antiseptic, apply bandage
- **Eye strain:** Take breaks, adjust screen brightness
- **Back pain:** Adjust seating, take breaks, stretch

**When to Seek Help:**
- Serious injuries
- Electrical shocks
- Burns
- Any situation requiring medical attention

## 4. Data and Cybersecurity | الأمان السيبراني

### 4.1 Data Protection

**Requirements:**
- ✅ Use strong passwords
- ✅ Keep software updated
- ✅ Backup important work regularly
- ✅ Use secure networks
- ✅ Protect sensitive data

**Best Practices:**
- Use password managers
- Enable two-factor authentication where possible
- Regular backups (daily for important work)
- Encrypt sensitive data
- Follow data privacy regulations (GDPR, etc.)

### 4.2 Network Safety

**Guidelines:**
- Use secure Wi-Fi networks
- Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive work
- Use VPN when accessing resources remotely
- Be cautious with file downloads
- Keep antivirus software updated

---

## 5. Physical Safety | السلامة البدنية

### 5.1 Ergonomics

**Workstation Setup:**
- Monitor at eye level
- Keyboard and mouse at comfortable height
- Feet flat on floor
- Back supported by chair
- Wrists straight when typing

**Break Schedule:**
- Take breaks every 60 minutes
- 5-10 minute breaks for every hour of work
- Look away from screen every 20 minutes
- Stretch and move around regularly

### 5.2 Health Considerations

**Eye Care:**
- Use proper lighting
- Adjust screen brightness
- Use blue light filters if needed
- Follow 20-20-20 rule (every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds)

**Posture:**
- Maintain good posture
- Adjust chair height
- Use ergonomic accessories if needed
- Take regular breaks
