## How to Use

1. **Start:** [README](README.md) → [Course 01/START_HERE](Course%2001/START_HERE.md) (or your course).
2. **Per unit:** Open the unit folder → `examples/` for notebooks, `exercises/` for practice.
3. **Full unit mapping:** See [UNIT_BY_UNIT_AUDIT](DOCS/guides/UNIT_BY_UNIT_AUDIT.md).
