# Unit 5: Ethics and Future Trends | الأخلاقيات والاتجاهات المستقبلية
## AIAT 124 - Generative AI

### Learning Objectives | أهداف التعلم

By the end of this unit, students will be able to:
- Understand ethical concerns in generative AI
- Detect and mitigate bias in generative models
- Address misinformation and deepfakes
- Implement responsible AI practices
- Explore future trends in generative AI

---

## Topics Covered | المواضيع المغطاة

Based on official curriculum (AIAT 124), this unit covers:

1. **Ethical Considerations**
   - Bias and fairness in generative AI
   - Representation issues
   - Cultural sensitivity
   - Privacy concerns
   - Consent and data usage

2. **Misinformation and Deepfakes**
   - Understanding deepfakes
   - Misinformation risks
   - Detection techniques
   - Mitigation strategies
   - Legal implications

3. **Intellectual Property**
   - Copyright issues
   - Ownership of AI-generated content
   - Training data rights
   - Fair use considerations
   - Licensing models

4. **Responsible AI Development**
   - Ethical guidelines
   - Transparency and explainability
   - Accountability
   - Safety measures
   - Human oversight

5. **Regulatory Landscape**
   - Current regulations
   - Industry standards
   - Compliance requirements
   - International perspectives
   - Future regulations

6. **Future Trends and Research**
   - Multimodal generation
   - Diffusion models advances
   - Large language models evolution
   - Real-time generation
   - Energy efficiency
   - Emerging applications
